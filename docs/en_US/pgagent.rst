.. _pgagent:


pgAgent
======= 

Introduced in pgAdmin III v1.4, pgAgent is a job scheduling agent for
PostgreSQL, capable of running multi-step batch/shell and SQL tasks on
complex schedules.

From pgAdmin 1.9 onwards, pgAgent is distributed independently of pgAdmin.
It may be downloaded from the `download area
<http://www.pgadmin.org/download>`_.
